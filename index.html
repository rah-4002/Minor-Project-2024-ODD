About Interusture

Hand gestures play a critical role in breaking barriers between individuals with hearing or speech impairments and the rest of the world. Our project, *Interusture*, leverages real-time gesture recognition to enable effective communication for deaf and blind individuals. By converting gestures into actionable interactions, we aim to foster inclusivity and empower those who face challenges in expressing themselves.

Through this innovative technology, we aspire to create a world where everyone can interact seamlessly, regardless of their physical limitations.
